DAVE BIO UNIVERSITY


You are now going to review 15 candidates for Dave Bio University. This university takes pride for their strong Biology department of the Medical program; hence, Biology is the most important criteria for them.

You will first have to make your initial choice by pressing "Accept" or "Reject". Then you will have to click on "Run the AI". DaveAI, university's admissions AI assistant, will give you its recommendation. Indicate your level of trust in its recommendation on a slider (0 - no trust at all; 100 - total trust). You may change your final decision by clicking "Accept" or "Reject". Press "Submit" to confirm your final decision.

DaveAI was trained on the admissions and university students' data of Dave Bio University from the previous years. Remember that any AI is only as good as the data and that candidates change from one year to another. So, it is possible that DaveAI would not give correct recommendations all the time for the candidates of 2019.
