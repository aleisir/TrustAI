MAVE CHEM UNIVERSITY


You are now going to review 15 candidates for Mave Chem University. This university takes pride for their strong Chemistry department of the Medical program; hence, Chemistry is the most important criteria for them.

You will first have to make your initial choice by pressing "Accept" or "Reject". Then you will have to click on "Run the AI". MaveAI, university's admissions AI assistant, will give you its recommendation. Then, you can change your initial decision by clicking on "Accept" or "Reject" again. Press "Submit" to confirm your final decision. Lastly, you must indicate your level of trust in its recommendation on a slider (0 - no trust at all; 100 - total trust).

MaveAI was trained on the admissions and university students' data of Mave Chem University from the previous years. Remember that any AI is only as good as the data and that candidates change from one year to another. So, it is possible that MaveAI would not give correct recommendations all the time for the candidates of 2019.
